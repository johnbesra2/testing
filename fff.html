About IITH Racing

The only thing that drives our car, is our Passion.


IITH Racing is a team of Engineering students from IIT Hyderabad who shares a passion for Automobiles and work into designing, building and testing an All-Terrain Car. Making ATV better and team stronger every year. This team was founded in year 2013 and since then, we have been participating in BAJA SAE India and competing against teams from all over India.




About BAJA SAE


BAJA SAE India is a student competition that involves designing, Engineering , Planing, Manufacturing and racing ALL-TERAIN Vehicles. The competition Goal include safety, good engineering practice, engineering design, and education of participating students. Each teams goal is to design and build aprototype of a rugged single seat, off-road recreational four wheel vehicle. It should be able to negotiate in all types of weather without any damage.
